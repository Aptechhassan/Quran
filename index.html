About Us

Welcome to Academic Quran

Online Quran Teaching] is the best online platform for learning the Quran and Islam. We offer a range of online Quran courses for kids and adults of all levels and backgrounds. Our online Quran courses include:

1 ) Quran Memorization: Learn how to memorize the Quran and become a Hafiz with our expert teachers.

2 ) Quran Recitation: Learn how to recite the Quran with proper pronunciation and Tajweed rules with our qualified teachers.

3 ) Quran Translation: Learn the meaning and message of the Quran in your own language with our experienced teachers.

4 ) Quran Tafseer: Learn the interpretation and explanation of the Quran with our knowledgeable teachers.

5 ) Dars e Nizami: Aalim course you can become an Aalim while taking classes from our highly qualified teachers.

6 ) Islamic Studies: Learn the basics of Islam and its teachings with our friendly teachers
